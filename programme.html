Programme 2025

Both Weekends

History Tour at DC presents the Moderne

DCTU presents an art-deco tour in the heart of Herne Hill, within a wider festival that takes part at the Dulwich Festival in 2025. Kick off the festival by joining the Herne Hill Society on 10th and 18th May at the court yard of Dorchester court. Entrance via Herne Hill main road.

  • Reserve Saturday 10th May AM slot !
  • Reserve Saturday 18th May PM slot !

Marianne Wie

Marianne Wie is an artist working within photography, cyanotype and sculpture. She will be exhibiting her work at the lobby of Block 8, SE24 9QY on the all days from 10 am - 17 pm.

Marianne Wie website

Second Weekend

Live Music! on Sunday 18th May

Music starts at 14:00

Join us for a semi-acoustic concert of resident musicians. We will be joined by the Brender Deluxe, Lou Terry and Bella. Bring your own drinks and snacks and enjoy the music in the courtyard.

Entrance via Herne Hill main road.

Dorchester Court Film & Art Project Second weekend 17th - 18th // 10am - 17pm

Meet the young resident artist group and their multi-media year-long work.
